About Me

My name is Katy Simpson. I'm new to coding but have always had a passion for technology since I was young. My father worked for a company that received tons of broken computers that sadly had to be tossed. I took those components and began to build "frankenstein" style computers. I built my first computer when I was just 10 years old out of junk material. As I grew older I mainly focused on the mechanical side of tech, working in manufacturing jobs in both military and healthcare related fields. It was after I started work at a local start up that I saw the impact that software and code could really do. After the economic downturn and massive layoff at my jobs I decided to make a career switch and self teaching myself code in hopes of one day turning my new found love of coding into a career.
